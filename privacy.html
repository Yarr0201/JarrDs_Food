Privacy Policy

Food AI respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data.

Information We Collect

The app may process the following data:

  • Food data entered manually or via photo
  • App usage data (for functionality and improvement)
  • Basic profile data (age, weight, goals)

We do NOT collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or location unless explicitly provided by you.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Camera — to analyze food images
  • Storage — to select images
  • Internet — to process AI requests

AI Processing

Some features use third-party AI services to analyze food and estimate nutritional values. Data sent to these services does not include personal identification.

Data Usage

Your data is used only to provide app functionality, including nutrition tracking and analysis.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Paid Services

The app may offer paid features or subscriptions. Payments are processed via third-party providers such as Stripe, YooKassa, or PayPal. We do not store your payment information.

Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device. Some data may be processed on secure servers for functionality.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time.
