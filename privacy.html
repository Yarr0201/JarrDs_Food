Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

NutriFly ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The app may process the following data:

  • Food data entered manually or via photo (ingredients, meal types, portion sizes)
  • App usage data (for functionality, crash reporting, and improvement)
  • Basic profile data (age, weight, height, activity level, fitness goals)
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version) for technical support

We do NOT collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise geolocation unless you explicitly provide them (e.g., when contacting customer support).

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Recognizing food items and calculating calories / macronutrients

  • Improving app accuracy, features, and user experience
  • Fixing bugs and analyzing app performance
  • Responding to your support requests

We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

You can change or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

Some features use third-party AI services to analyze food images and estimate nutritional values. Data sent to these services:

We do NOT store your payment card or banking information. The payment provider may collect billing details according to its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their policies before making a payment.

Data Storage & Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Most data (profile, food logs, history) is stored locally on your device
  • Some data may be processed on encrypted servers with restricted access
  • We use secure connections (TLS/SSL) for all network communications
  • Access to servers is limited to authorized personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the app's functionality. Specifically:
